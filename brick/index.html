Gyro Blocks

Quick setup for two-gyro play:

    • Device A — Move: First click 🔍 Scan, then 🔗 Connect, and finally 📡 Stream. Rotate around the Z axis to move the block left and right
    • Device B — Rotate: Same steps (Scan → Connect → Stream) with your second device. Rotate around the Z axis to rotate the block.

Requirements:

  • Device: Shimmer3R
  • Firmware: Version ≥ v1.0.22

🎮 Gyro Blocks — Dual BLE (Move + Rotate)

Use two Shimmer devices: one controls left/right movement; the other controls rotation. Keyboard fallback works too.
